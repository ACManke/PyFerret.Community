Ferret allows full control over contour levels. Briefly you could do the
following:

contour/lev=(100,11000,100) topo

Which will draw all the contour intervals from 100 to 11000 with intervals of
100.

For complete reference including changing line types and pen colors, refer to
the ferret manual Chapter 8, page 108.
