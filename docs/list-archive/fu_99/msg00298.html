Hi,

In mp_stereographic_south.jnl it says almost at the end:
let/quiet y_page = (-1.0) * mp_rho * cos(mp_lambda-mp_lambda0) ! eqn
(109)
which is exactly the same as in mp_stereographic_north.jnl .
                    ^
I changed this to
let/quiet y_page = (1.0) * mp_rho * cos(mp_lambda-mp_lambda0) ! eqn
(109)
                    ^
Now the longitudes are no longer flipped on the southern hemisphere.
