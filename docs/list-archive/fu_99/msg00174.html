Hi Ilana,

In addition to the grayscale options, Ferret V5 also has
	/PATTERN=
options, which allow you to use hatch patterns of various type for
filling areas. The hatches make excellent (Xerox) reproductions in black
and white -- better than do grayscales.

	- steve

P.S. I believe that the "Fpattern" command, which allows you to ask what
pattern files exist, was omitted from the V5 BETA release. You can find
it on the downloads page
(http://ferret.wrc.noaa.gov/Ferret/Downloads/ferret_downloads.html).
Look under "binary script files".
