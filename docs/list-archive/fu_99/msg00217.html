Unfortunately, this is something I often have to solve by trial and error, but
what usually works for me is the @FNR transformation. This should be in SHOW
TRANSFORMATION, but it definitely is detailed in the manual in chapter 3,
section 2.4.25 (pg. 52, of version 4.4 of the user guide). So the correct line
would be:

let neplow = nep[g=newgrid,y=@FNR:1]

This is assuming that you are missing only one point of latitude at the top and
bottom, change the FNR:1 to whatever is appropriate.
