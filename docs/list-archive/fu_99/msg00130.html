    Thanks Steve, your idea works fine.

    The problem was that I couldn't remove labels properly in my script
and the solution Steve send me is:

> set region/x=-1:0.6/y=38:40.2/l=98
>
> ! THIS MAKES YOUR BASE CONTOUR MAP WITH THE UNWANTED LABELS REMOVED
>
> contour/SET_UP/levels=(0,275,25) totpcp
>
> go unlabel 1
> go unlabel 2
> go unlabel 3
> go unlabel 5
> ppl CONTOUR
>
> go land thick
>
> label 0,40.03,0,0,.15 @P1Castello
> label 0,40,0,0,.2 @P1*
>
    My problem came from the SET_UP in the contour line, I didn't put it
and the position of the ppl contour on the script.

    Hope this will help someone else.
