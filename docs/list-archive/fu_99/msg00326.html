Dear Zhang,

To use MOM output directly, you must output your results in netcdf
format. First install netcdf and udunits from www.unidata.ucar.edu,
turn on the netcdf option in the compile cpp options (-Dnetcdf).

For more information look at the MOM manual.

Swathi
