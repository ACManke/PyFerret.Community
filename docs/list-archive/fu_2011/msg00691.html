Isn't that what XBOX and YBOX do?

I guess you'd have to scale by cos(latitude) if your grid is in degrees.

let pi=4*atan(1)
let cell_area = xbox[g=gridname]*ybox[g=gridname]*cos(pi*y[gy=gridname]/180)
