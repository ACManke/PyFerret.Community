Dear Szymon,
you may use fill_xy(data, mask, 2) before you filter data. This extrapolates the data smoothly into land by two points and the filter @SBX and @SHN can do something reasonable near land too. Applying your land mask later removes remaining extrapolated points. 

Greetings,
Martin
