Hi Rob,
Have a look at the @LOC and @WEQ transformations.  There are examples of say, computing the location of the 20-degree isotherm, which might be similar to what you're thinking; you would define your variable and do a contour plot. There's also an FAQ,
How can I show the value of my variable at its deepest depth?

! get array of diffs
LET xdiff = (-1)*R1[X=@DDC] ! negative so I can visualise downwards
