The oceanographic community no longer uses permille for salinity. We now use dimensionless salinity according to PSS-78 (e.g. http://www.seabird.com/FAQs/FAQsGeneralOceanographic.htm) . Most papers identify salinity with the unit "psu" (practical salinity unit). This can be controversial, with some people arguing that salinity has no units and should be shown as a pure number. I am not among these people, as I think "psu" is clearer, and certainly it is easy to write in Ferret. But I think most reviewers would complain about salinity cited in permille in text or figures to be published.
