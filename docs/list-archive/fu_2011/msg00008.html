Jian Ma wrote:

> The attached figure is often seen in many publications. Can ferret do
> that? I tried to use "fill" with self-defined variable but the plot
> resolution is too low.
> 
> I would appreciate very much if you can provide some easy and feasible
> script to do it.

Try the fill_between script (I just found that one last week).

define axis /x=0:10:0.1 xax

let center = sin(x[gx=xax])
let top = center + 0.02*x[gx=xax]
let bottom = center - 0.02*x[gx=xax]

go fill_between poly/nolab/pal=gray bottom xax top
plot /overlay /col=red/thick=3 center
