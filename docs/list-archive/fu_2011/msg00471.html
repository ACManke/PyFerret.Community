Such a plot is really 2 entirely separate plots.

! first contour density as a function of T and S
! this establishes the axes
contour rho_un(x[gx=34:37:.1],y[gy=2:30:.1],0)-1000

! then overlay your particular T-S curve or curves
plot/vs/over temperature,salinity
