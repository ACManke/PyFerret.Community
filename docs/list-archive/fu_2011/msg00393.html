Title: Removing axis and plotting vectors outside of plot region
Dear Ferret users,

We use Ferret to produce the plots at:

    http://www.mbari.org/oasis/m1/contour.tstring.html

During this time of the year we have strong upwelling favorable winds along the central Califronia coast. The full magnitude of these winds are difficult to see in the plot because the vectors are clipped at the right-hand edge of the plot.
