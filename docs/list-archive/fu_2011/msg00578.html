Hi,

One further thing. Are you really wanting to restrict your time region from 1-jan-1979 to 1-dec-2006, i.e. the end of November?
If you don't have data for Dec 2006 then fine but you may really want to define your variables as 

let uu=uwnd[d=1,t=1-jan-1979:1-jan-2007]

etc.

Russ
