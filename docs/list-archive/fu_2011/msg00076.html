txlint,low,high time axis lab intervals (0,1=yr only; 1,0=mon only; 3,1=every 3 mon+yr) 
txnmtc,icode		icode=-1 for large tics at year breaks
txtype,type,style type=days,mon,yr(def); style=hr,hrday/day,daymon/ mon1,mon3,monyr
(Find better descriptions in the PPLUS manual). There's a lot of control over these tics.
