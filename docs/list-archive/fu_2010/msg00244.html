On 8 April 2010 00:28, David Wang <dwang@xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx> wrote:
Since the time axes of your datasets are degenerate, alternatively you can remove the time axes before subtraction by using

fill INTRA_...[d=2,l=1:1@max] - INTRA_...[d=1,l=1:1@max]

David
