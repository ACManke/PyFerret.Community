Yogesh,
The FRAME/FILE=name.gif (in x-windows mode, not starting Ferret with ferret -gif) just captures what's on the screen, so another alternative would be to just do that yourself with the PrintScreen key and and pasting into a free software program such as IrfanView (where you would have to do just a bit to the image, such as removing the windows border). Then you can save the gif image and use it as you wish.
