On Tue, 2010-05-11 at 23:38 -0600, Paul Young wrote:
> Hi Prescilla,
> 
> You can refer to T by using quotation marks (can't remember whether  
> double or single, and can't find in the documentation at the mo). E.g.
> 
> fill/l=10/k=1 "T"
> 
> Of course, you can also rename the variable with nco operators (http://nco.sourceforge.net/ 
> ). E.g.
> 
> ncks -v T infile.nc just_T.nc   							!dump temperature from large file
> ncrename -v T,temperature just_T.nc					!rename T to temperature
> 
> Hope this helps
> 
> Paul
