Hi Patrick,
What you're seeing is the effect of the length of February, 28.2425 days, its long-term average length in the Gregorian calendar. 5:49:12 is 24 hours * 0.2425.  This varying-length month means the calculations look messy, but this definition is what is needed for consistent monthly modulo regridding using this calendar.

Ansley
