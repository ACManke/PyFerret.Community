On Thu, Sep 23, 2021 at 5:51 AM Ansley Manke <a.c.manke@xxxxxxxxx> wrote:

Hi,

I agree that your definition for the mask looks correct. What does a plot of "shade mask_var1" look like?

I don't know what might be causing what you are seeing. Is the variable var1 on exactly the same grid as rose from etopo5?  Even if both grids have the same resolution, maybe the grid cells are not quite defined the same way in both grids. Do you see NOTE's about "ambiguous coordinates" when Ferret draws the plot of rose*mask_var1? That would mean it was able to work with the two grids together in one _expression_, but that the coordinates were not the same in some way.

It would be a good idea to be sure that the grids are an exact match with a regridding operation:

   shade mask_var1[d=1, gxy=rose[d=2]]* rose[d=2]
