You may use Vector command like :
VECTOR/i=1:20/j=1:20/len=15 i+cos(j/5)+5,i-j
 [ More on this here ]
So for your variable [e.g. u and v] you may do VECTOR/i=1:20/j=1:20/len=10 u,v  [adjust arguments as per your choice]
