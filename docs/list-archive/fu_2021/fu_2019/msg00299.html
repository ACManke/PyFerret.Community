Ferret/PyFerret does units conversions and takes distances on the earth into account when the grid units are well defined. Please see this documentation: https://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/users-guide/variables-xpressions/XPRESSIONS#GeneralInfo

It also discusses multi-directional transformations; including the cases where area-averaging is used
