Hi Pepijn, Geoff, and Emilio,

You are right. Ferret version 4.5 does not support color displays of greater
than 8 bit depth. Ferret version 4.9, which we are in the process of packaging
and documenting now, will fix this problem. Version 4.9 will be available very
soon ...

	- steve
