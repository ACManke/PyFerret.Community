Hi Jimmy,
When the palette is defined as a percent palette you need to make the first column of the palette go up to 100 % - maybe just add the line

    0.0  100.0  100.0  100.0
   10.0   95.0   95.0   95.0
   20.0   90.0   90.0   90.0
   35.0   82.0   82.0   82.0
   45.0   76.0   76.0   76.0
   60.0   69.0   69.0   69.0
   65.0   70.0   70.0   70.0
   80.0   90.0   90.0   90.0
  100.0   90.0   90.0   90.0
or whatever you think is appropriate for the color scale you want.
