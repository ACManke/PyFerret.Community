Hi Jimmy,
You can combine the -batch and -script startup options, exactly as you did:

 ferret -batch file.ps -script script.jnl 1 2
The only thing to be careful, and you did it correctly, of is to specify -script as the last startup option with its arguments following. What happened when you tried this? I don't quite know what you mean by the log file. What version of Ferret are you running and on what platform?
