Hi all,

I am running a model with 15 depth levels (non-uniform and in reverse order, so i use the K index) and plotting each slice of ocean temperature/salinity etc with a repeat statement:

use state.0004248000.glob.nc
set region/x=180w:180w/y=90s:90n/l=1
REPEAT/K=15:1:-1 (shade/palette=ocean_temp temp; shade/over/nolabel/noaxes/lev="(0)(10000)"/pal=gray depth; FRAME/FILE=temp_slice.gif)

At the moment, the continents are masked out as they appear at the sea surface and I know i can get the correct mask for each slice if I change /lev"(0)(10000)" to /lev="(-2500)(10000)" but is there a way to do this within a repeat ie /lev"(Z(k))(10000)" ?? I just get "Error Syntax error"
