Hi Kirsty,
            There is no "one command" procedure to get a logarithmic color
key, but there are workarounds. Please have a look at following postings in 
the archive:

1. http://www.pmel.noaa.gov/maillists/tmap/ferret_users/fu_2004/msg00442.html
2. http://www.pmel.noaa.gov/maillists/tmap/ferret_users/fu_2004/msg00427.html

and this FAQ 

 http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/FERRET_17sep07/FAQ/custom_plots/2D_log_plot.html

The GO file described in 1. above will be a convenient solution, however,
you may have to modify it slightly according to your requirements.
