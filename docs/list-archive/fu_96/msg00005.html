Regarding UWM/COADS (also known as the daSilva COADS data) and Ferret: 

I've downloaded many of the daSilva data sets from the LDEO Climate Group's
WWW Climate Library in netCDF format and have found them to be directly
usable in Ferret (ie, the data is ordered correctly). The URL is: 

http://rainbow.ldgo.columbia.edu/~benno/ingrid/intro/index.html

There are probably other sources, but this one has worked well for me.
