Hi Anne,

	Maybe it's not directly ferret, but others might be interested. I don't
know your exact need, but there are a number of CD-ROM data sets that should
fill your needs. For example:

1) NOAA/NGDC (National Geophysical Data Center) Marine Geological and
Geophysical Data from NGDC - Available from NGDC Boulder, CO

2) NOAA/NGDC Global Relief Data - Also available from NGDC Boulder, CO

3) GEBCO Digital Atlas - Available from the British Oceanographic Data Centre,
UK

Some of these might be directly available to you, try the UW libraries (esp.
Oceanography) and maybe the library at PMEL.

	Good Luck, Mark
