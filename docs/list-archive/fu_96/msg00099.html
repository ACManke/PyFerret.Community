This is a question that I have had also. In my case it's a little different in
that some of us do get windows, and some don't. We are all running both ferret
V4.11 and V4.30 on Silicon Graphics under IRIX 5.3. Thus, it must be something
in the X-windows setup.

On another topic - a warning. Ferret will not currently run under IRIX 6.2, it
appears to be caused by the new revision of X-windows (rev. 6). So if you are
on SGI's and want to keep running ferret, leave one machine at IRIX 6.1 or
below. I am working with Jerry Davison on this, though we might have to wait
until Steve Hankin get's back from travel.
