Dear Domingos,
                            You need to set that in your xorg.conf file as below under Section Device. I am having Ubuntu and in my case it is in  /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "Configured Video Device"
        Option "BackingStore" "True"

hope this helps
