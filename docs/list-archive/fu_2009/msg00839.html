I see.  I misread your initial message.  If you want both shading and
contours, you need:

   use levitus_climatology
   shade/k=1 temp
   contour/k=1/ov temp

Notice the "ov" qualifier on the second line.
It tells Ferret to "overlay" contours on the
existing plot.
