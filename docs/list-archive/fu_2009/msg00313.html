During the installation, I encountered a tiny problem.
Finstall looks for "fer_executables.tar.Z" but the file
is distributed as a "fer_executables.tar.gz".
A workaround is

   $ sed 's/\.tar\.Z/.tar.gz/g' Finstall > Finstall-linux
