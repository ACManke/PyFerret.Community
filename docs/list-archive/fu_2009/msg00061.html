Ferreters,

I'd like to report that a netCDF file with
a global title of "@@@TITLE@@@" causes Ferret to crash.

  
  $ ferret
        NOAA/PMEL TMAP
        FERRET v6.02
        Linux(g77) 2.4.21-32 - 04/30/07
         9-Feb-09 16:33

  set mode grat:dash
  yes? set data tmp2.nc
  yes? fill u1
  Segmentation fault

  $ ncdump -h tmp2.nc
  . . . . . .
  // global attributes:
                  :title = "@@@TITLE@@@" ;


When the title is replaced with "my title" (using ncatted),
Ferret doesn't crash.

Patient: Doctor, it hurts when I do this.
Doctor:  Don't do that!

I know, and I don't use such a title as that
(which was accidentally created).  I just wanted
to report the crash.

Regards,
Ryo
