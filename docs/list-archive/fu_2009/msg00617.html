On Sep 18, 2009, at 1:13 PM, Stephen Guimond wrote:
Regarding the fusing of polygons, would interpolating the data to a very high resolution grid in space and time alleviate some of the aliasing issues? Either that or smooth the variable beforehand?
Perhaps, but it would have the negative effect of removing contour labels, which are often missing in plots with very dense grids. The algorithm is looking for places where there is physical room to write a label, and with a very dense grid the blocks become very small, and none of them have enough room for the label.
