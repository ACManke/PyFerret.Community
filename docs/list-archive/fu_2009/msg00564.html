Hi Patrick,
In addition, Ferret doesn't understand the French term Février. The following works in Ferret v6.2 on linux: 
def axis/t="01-JAN-1860":"30-FEB-1890":1/edges/units="Months"/cal=360_day simutaxis2

Fabian
