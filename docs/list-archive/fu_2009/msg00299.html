Hi Nidia,
Sometime when you have an example that is not working, try doing a "set mode diag" before the regridding command.  Don't worry about the large amount of output, but just try to read it through, and look at the index ranges of data as it's loaded.  Sometimes this helps to turn up what's happening.

There's an FAQ about diagnostic mode,

How do I interpret the output of "SET MODE DIAGNOSTIC?"
http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/FERRET_17sep07/FAQ/other/mode_diagnostic.html
