Hi Madhaven,

A good way to make symbols at particular locations on a plot is the "go polymark" script.  Here is a demo that shows how to call it:  http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/ferret-polymark-demo

The demo shows stars and squares, but the script can be called with a variety of shapes.  Type

yes? go/help polymark

to see a the documentation that is at the start of the script.  It lists the shapes that are available.
