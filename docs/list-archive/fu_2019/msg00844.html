Dear PyFerreters,

For more than 20 years Ansley Manke has been a key developer on Ferret and PyFerret, and a constant contributor to the Ferret support list. While she retired from full-time work a year ago, Ansley continued part time development and user-list support during the past year. On September 30 2020, Ansley will finally “officially” retire from Ferret development and user support.


We’d like to thank Ansley for her years of invaluable contributions towards the development, maintenance and support of PyFerret, and wish her well in her retirement. 


Support for PyFerret continues at NOAA/PMEL, with Joshua Osborne taking the lead in development and support with the PMEL Science Data Integration Group


Yours,

  The PyFerret team
