Dear golla nageswararao,

> Can anybody tell me how to convert the pressure coordinates to height
> coordinates in ferret?

this depends on the type of data file you have to begin with, and what
you want to do (plots or calculations).  In general the function
zaxreplace does what you need, check out the manual at

http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/release-notes/users-guide/variables-xpressions/XPRESSIONS

(scroll down to ZAXREPLACE)  or checkout the topic

http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/faq/depth-axis-to-density-axis/?searchterm=zaxreplace

(Depth axis to Density axis)

The problem of depth v.s. density is similar as height v.s. pressure.
