The "COLUMNS" qualifer will recognize dates.

http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/users-guide/commands-reference/SET#_VPINDEXENTRY_1610

So, without any special /delimiter qualifier, the 01/01/00  etc will be read in as dates. Although I have not checked how years of 00 are translated; I think they will translate to 2000.  Selvam, are those intended to be year 2000, or year 0, or what?  I would try,

columns/var="tvar, var"/type="date"  read.txt

Then you could define a time axis from tvar and assign the data to that time axis.
