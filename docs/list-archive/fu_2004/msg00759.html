Hi Ramesh,
            Hope by polar co-ordinate you mean curvilinear grid. Ferret offers
utilities for visualization and regridding (to regular rectilinear grid) 
of curvilinear data. If you can handle ascii data in Ferret, please have a 
look at the following references:

visualization :
---------------
     Three-argument (curvilinear) version of SHADE, FILL, CONTOUR, and  VECTOR
         Ch6 Sec10.  MAP PROJECTIONS AND CURVILINEAR COORDINATES 

regridding    :
---------------     
http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/Mail_Archives/fu_2003/msg01347.html


 If you need any more clarification please post back all the relevant details
and a subset of your ascii data.


With Regards

Jaison
