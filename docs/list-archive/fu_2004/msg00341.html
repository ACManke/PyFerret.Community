As of May 7th, NOAA has mandated that the pmel.noaa.gov addresses may no longer
be used. Our computer administrators have been able to use forwarding in an interim

PLEASE remove any references you may have to ferret_users@ferret.pmel.noaa.gov,
and change to oar.pmel.ferret_users@noaa.gov .  This address is on the Mail Archive
web page.  Also note that if you do a "REPLY ALL" to a message that was sent
earlier to the older address, your reply will disappear.

Thank you,  the Ferret Developers.
