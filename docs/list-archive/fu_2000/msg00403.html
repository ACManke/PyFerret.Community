Hello,

Further to my recent email concerning removing the year labels, I have
solved the problem myself using:

ppl txlint, 1, 0

which keeps the lowest intervals (in the case given months) and removes
the higher time interval (years). Hope this helps other people who have
come across the same problem,

Graham Gladman
