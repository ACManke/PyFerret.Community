	First, there are no stupid questions only stupid answers, and I
hope that this one isn't (stupid that is).

	Always check the underlying grid definition to see the
consequences of your actions.

yes? show grid kkk

or better would be:

yes? show grid/l=1 kkk

Which will tell you what the underlying units are for your time axis. My
guess is that your underlying unit is HOUR, and thus to get 10 days you
need only use 10*24 so your LET would be:

let kkkregrid=kkk[t=1-jan-1960:1-jan-1997:240]
