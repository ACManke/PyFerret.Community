Mail 1:

Mail 2:
> In case you are interested, to get the movies to play on windows
> machines, it seems that magnitude of IQSCALE, PQSCALE, and
> BQSCALE is crucial.  If they are too small, the movie will not
> play. 

Microsoft's default MPEG player has problems with my MPEG movies, but a
free MPEG player called VMPEG (as noted by Chittibabu) plays them well
(with those scales set to 1). You can download it from
ftp://ftp.colorado.edu/cns/pc/Win95/HelperApps/vmpeg17.exe. I don't use
Windows, and therefore did no more than verify that VMPEG works. I cannot
say if it will work out of powerpoint.

Regards, Shankar.

P.S.: I've not yet seen a Win2000 machine; my test of VMPEG was on
Win 95/98.
