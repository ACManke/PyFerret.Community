Dear Golla,

On Fri, 17 May 2013 11:55:47 +0530 golla nageswararao wrote:
> Hi all,
>  Does any body knows how to calculate dot product between two vectors in
> ferret?

You can do this for two vectors V1 and V2:

? let A = V1*V2
? list A[i=@sum]

since the vector product is the summed elements of the direct product.
