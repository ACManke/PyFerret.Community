Hello Christelle,

try using a bit longer version of go land, i.e.
yes? GO land black overlay black !this overlays your map with country-boundaries

Interestingly, I cannot see SST among your variables, are you sure it is there?

Also,
yes? sh grid SST
would give us a better view on your longitudes and latitudes dimensions. This might be also a source of the "land appears all squeezed in the bottom of" your plot.

Peter
