The error you get is exactly what the error message states.
The grave accent evaluation works only on a scalar:

yes? let v = {1, 4, 9, 16}
yes? say `v`
**ERROR: invalid command: grave accent doesnt evaluate to scalar
          v
yes? let u = 2
yes? say `u`
!-> MESSAGE/CONTINUE 2
2
yes?
