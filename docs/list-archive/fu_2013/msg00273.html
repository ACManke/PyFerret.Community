As a workaround:

Make things explicit step by step ....

let new_ax=x[gx=sh, i=10:100]
define grid/x=new_axis/y=sh new_grid
let new_sh=sh[g=new_grid]
shade new_sh
save/clobber/file=test.nc new_sh

Hope this helps for the moment ....
