Hello all,
NOAA has been making a transition to a set of new email
addresses, changing from name@pmel.noaa.gov to addresses
like First.M.Last@noaa.gov.  During the transition, mail to
the old pmel.noaa.gov addresses has been forwarded to the
new ones, however the old addresses will be retired this month.

This affects individual email addresses and also addresses for
the Ferret Users' List and other contact addresses.

If you have Ferret and other NOAA addresses in your address
books, please change them.  The new addresses for general
contacts can be found in our web pages, i.e. the the Ferret Users'
List page at
http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/Mail_Archives/ferret_mail_archives.html

and also the "contact us" page at

http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/ferret_contact_us.html

If you need help finding the correct address for somebody
at PMEL, I would be happy to help.

Ansley Manke
