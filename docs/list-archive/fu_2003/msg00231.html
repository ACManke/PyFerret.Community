Hi Barry,
Those are informational messages, not errors.  The unit MONTH is
inherently ambiguous.  One might mean the middle of each calendar
month, which results in an irregularly-spaced time axis because the
months are of different length.  Or you might want an axis with the
year equally divided into 12 equal time steps.  By default, the latter
is what Ferret uses.

Ferret has some tools to help you create a monthly climatology.  There
are pre-defined regular and irregular monthly climatological time axes
available.  Please see "climatology, creating", in the Users Guide, or the
FAQ, "How do I calculate climatologies and climatological anomalies?",
at http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/FAQ/analysis/climatologies.html
for details and examples.

Ansley
