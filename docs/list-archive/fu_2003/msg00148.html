Hi Frank,
Ferret has a maximum line length of 3600 characters for tab-delimited
files.  You can get most of the same information with a fortran-format
specification and the /HEADING=enhanced qualifier. Use a format
appropriate to your data, but for example:

LIST/FILE=out.dat/FORMAT=(10F8.3)/HEADING=enhanced  var

This outputs the coordinates and then the variable values.

Ansley Manke
