Hello Arne and David -

I believe that the reason you are seeing the gksm2ps problem that you describe is due to the fact that the most recent version of Ferret had been built on a Tru64 v5.0 machine.  The binaries built under v5.0 of Tru64 seem to be incompatible with v4.0 of Tru64.  

I have rebuilt both Ferret v5.50 and gksm2ps under Tru64 v4.0 and updated the fer_executables.tar.Z file in the OSF Ferret download area.   Downloading this file and reinstalling the binaries should solve your problems with gksm2ps.

Please let me know if you have any other questions.

Kevin
