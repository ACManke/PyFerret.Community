Hi Sudheer,

I don't see an error message below, only a warning that the term "month" is ambiguous; in general it could mean 30 days, 31 days, or 365.2425/12 days, etc. Ferret is telling you that it is assuming the latter.

For instance, 2002 and 2003 were not leap years, so you might consider using 365/12 days, as in:
DEFINE AXIS/T="5-Jan-2002":"30-Oct-2003":`365/12`/unit=days t_month

Does that help?

Rob
