Hi all,

This is just a friendly reminder to please use a subject line when writing to the Ferret Users List.  A subject that is as specific as possible will help others determine if they can contribute to the question.  Please avoid writing questions with no subject line or with something very general, like "ferret question" in the subject.

Other general suggestions for good questions are on the Users' List page.

Thank you!
