You can use the zsequence command to create your irregular vertical axis and then regrid the data to your required region. I hope this can help you.

let depth=ZSEQUENCE({`1`,`5`,`10`,`15`,`30`,`45`,`60`,`70`,`100`,`150`,`200`}
