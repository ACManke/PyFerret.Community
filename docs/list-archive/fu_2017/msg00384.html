Hi,
You can define a time axis that is not related to dates on a calendar. It will still give you the NOTE, which is saying Ferret doesn't automatically do anything with units of pentads. 

   DEFINE AXIS/T=1:19:1/units=pentads  taxis_new_pentads
Then the axis will be labeled with simple numbers, and the axis label will be the variable's title and the units of (pentad).
