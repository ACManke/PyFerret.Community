Hi Vincent,
There are several sampling functions in Ferret, one of which will help you. Take a look at SAMPLEXY and SAMPLEXY_CURV. Or say
yes? show function samplexy* 
to see them all. Which one you need depends on what the grid of the data is like.

If you need a hand figuring out how to use one of these, then please write back - Is the 2D field on a rectilinear grid?  Or do the longitudes and latitudes represent a curvilinear grid?  If you open the datasets, what does the output of SHOW DATA look like?

Ansley
