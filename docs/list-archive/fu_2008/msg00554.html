Hi Jamml,
You can define the variables you are plotting, giving them titles. Then each variable's title is listed in the legend at the bottom of the plot

   yes? let/title="Station 1" svar1 = svar[x=178,y=34]
   yes? let/title="Station 2" svar2 = svar[x=172,y=14]

   yes? plot svar1, svar2

The titles Station 1 and Station 2 will be the labels on the line segments
