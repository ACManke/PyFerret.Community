Kamoru,

Would you give us more information about instalation steps?

Maybe you're trying to call csh-version of ferret_paths through a bash session. In this case, try to source your ferret_paths after entered a csh session:

> csh
> source /usr/local/ferret_paths
> ferret

Particularly, I prefer to setup a bash-version ferret_paths.
