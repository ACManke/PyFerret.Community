Hello Xin,
You can use "index-stamping"  to do this kind of thing.  Define
a new, axis, say in the K direction.  Then

LET combined = IF k eq 1 THEN var1 ELSE var2

from which combined[k=1] gives var1 and combined[k=2] gives
var2.

Ansley Manke
