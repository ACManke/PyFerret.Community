Hi Nicholas,
	Its best to stay away from special characters in the character
fonts.  There are SYMBOL fonts that allow you to do a lot, and in
particular the Math Font @ma

	For your label try
		@ma22@srW/m^2@ma23

(i.e.  shift to math font and draw character 22 which is [
       go back to @sr (or your choice) to do the W/m and superscript 2
       return to math font for character 23 which is ]
