Hi Steve,

The DEFINE AXIS command defines the coordinate axis of your data -- the locations of data points.  It does not describe the appearance of a physical axis drawn on a plot. 

See "Chapter 6: CUSTOMIZING PLOTS -- 3. Axes" in the Ferret Users Guide at http://www.ferret.noaa.gov/Ferret/Documentation/Users_Guide/current/fer_html.htm for a discussion of customizing the axes of a  plot.  You may, however, need to resort to manual labels on your axis to get 100% control over which tic marks get labeled.

    - Steve
