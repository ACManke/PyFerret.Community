Yep, this sounds familiar to me. I couldn't find a log palette either BUT the trick is to specify the intervals in the levels:

fill/line/key*/lev=(0.01,0.05,0.01)(0.05,0.5,0.05)(0.5,1,0.1)(1,8,1)*/title="CSat Annual Mean (1998-2004)" csat_annual; go land

Now it should look like a logarithmic palette.
I hope this helps!
