Hi Hein,
The messages you're seeing from Ferret are not an error, just a "Note" to let you know that Ferret doesn't do any internal conversions with Hertz. Ferret will store the units of Hertz, use them to label your plots, and write the units to output files, but it does not do any internal conversions using them as it does for units of time and distance on coordinate axes.

Ansley
