Hi Nadia,
To control the spacing of the tic marks and numeric labels on axes, you can use the /HLIMITS and /YLIMITS qualifiers on your plot command. For instance, compare the plots for these two commands

yes? plot/i=1:100 sin(i/20)
yes? plot/i=1:100/vlim=-1.2:1.2/hlim=0:100:20 sin(i/20)

The argument to /HLIM or /VLIM may have either the upper and lower limits, or the upper, lower, and an increment.
