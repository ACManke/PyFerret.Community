Hi Paul,
Ferret is not set up to process the words -inf and inf for levels in a palette file. Interesting that it works at all. It's a good idea to make it do so, and I've put it on our list of future improvements. You get a prize for that, and also the pretty-palette-of-the-week award.

Ansley

with converting *.plt to *.ps....
I have made my own colour palette to match the NASA lidar ozone plots
(see http://asd-www.larc.nasa.gov/lidar/data/tra13no1.gif for example).

the postscript file (something like $ sed 's/NaN/1.0/g' in.ps > out.ps)

a prize for spotting it!?
