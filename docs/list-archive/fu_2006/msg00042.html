GO GRATICULE

will draw a grid on the plot and omit the ticks.

GO TICS

cancels (back to default.

Also try

WHERE

Then click your mouse at the spot you want, and the coordinates will be written to the command window. A note of caution on WHERE: On at least some systems (Sun Solaris), this only works in window number 1, and Ferret will hang until the mouse is clicked in window 1. On Macs, WHERE doesn't appear to work at all.
