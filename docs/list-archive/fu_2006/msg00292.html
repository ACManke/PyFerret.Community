Your script is making 2 plots. To avoid this, use /SET:

plot/SET//thick=2 var1,var2,var3
go unlabel ....
ppl plot

Also, don't forget PPL CLSPLT at the end to finish the metafile.

BK

On Apr 30, 2006, at 7:20 PM, zhen.li@gsfc.nasa.gov wrote:
Hi Ferret users:

>>>>>

go unlabel 4
