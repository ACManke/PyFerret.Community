On Thu, Nov 02, 2006 at 04:04:54PM -0800, william s. kessler wrote:
> Hi XiangDong -
> 
> When you specify the levels, use -3 for the key (4th value in the  
> level specification):
> 
> contour/lev=(0,10,1,-3) variable	! 0 to 10 by 1 with no label
> 
> Other possibilities
> 
> contour/lev=(0,10,1,-1) variable	! 0 to 10 by 1 with integer labels
> 
> contour/lev=(0,10,1,-1)(4.75,5.75,.5,2) variable	! 0 to 10 by 1 with  
> integer labels, plus 4.75 to 5.75 by 0.5 with 2 decimal places in the  
> label
> 
> Various other possibilities exist, for example control of which are  
> dashed or thick lines. This should be given under contour in the manual.
>
