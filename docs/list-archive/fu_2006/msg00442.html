Hi Jozef,
      Use "veckey" command to sort out your problem.

for example:
 vector/x=50:100/y=5:10/set/length=0.5  U,V          ! here keeping "set" is must, this will open vectors mannual setup
 ppl veckey Xpos,Ypos                                              ! this will put the key at your desired location.
 vector                                                                 ! this is to end vector plotting.

i thing this will solve your problem. All the best
