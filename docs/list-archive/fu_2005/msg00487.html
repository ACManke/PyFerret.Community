Use xbox and zbox:

let meri_transp_box = v*xbox*zbox

If your grid is geographical then this will be transport across each 
gridbox of your section in units of m3/s.

A test is whether meri_transp_box[x=@sum,z=@sum] equals the total 
you found by your original method.
