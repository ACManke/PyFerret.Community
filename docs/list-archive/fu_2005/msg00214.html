Hi Ming,
You can define a new axis in Ferret, and use the @ASN or @NRST regridding
transformation to put your data onto it.

yes? define axis/x=1:400:1  xax
yes? let new_var = var[gx=xax@asn]
yes? list/i=1:5 new_var

This puts the data that had been at x=0.5 on the new axis, at x=1.

Ansley
