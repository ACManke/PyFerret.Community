On Tue, 2005-05-24 at 10:08, Ansley Manke wrote:
> Hi all,
> Emilie and I looked at this more, and it looks like a bug that resets 
> the pen used for key labels when the SHADE/SET or FILL/SET command is 
> used.  Emilie found a workaround: using  /COLOR=7 on the plotting 
> command forces the pen choice to remain at 7.
> 
>   ppl pen, 0, 7
>   shade/color=7/set var
>   ppl xlab " new label"
>   ppl shade
>
