Hi Ashraf,
Welcome to Ferret! We do not have the /THICK qualifier on contour plots, only 1-dimensional line plots. We should have added this option as well as the /COLOR qualifier, and I will put this on our list to add! For now you will need to use a /PEN qualifier instead,

/PEN=1 thin black
/PEN=2 thin red
...

/PEN=7 medium black
/PEN=8 medium red
...
/PEN=13 thick black
/PEN=14 thick red
...
