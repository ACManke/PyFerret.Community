So many problem little problems like changing line thickness are trivial in Adobe Illustrator. Why not just make your plot in ferret, then read it into Adobe Illustrator (if you have this software) and modify it as you see fit?

Regarding histogram plot: (These are actually more generally called bar charts or bar graphs, although your confusion is very understandable because the more narrowly defined histograms (like a discrete version of probability density) are almost always drawn as bar charts.)

Ferret v5.6 has a few go files, bar_chart1, bar_chart2, and bar_chart3.
