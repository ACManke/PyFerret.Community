Hi Jammal,
This is described in Chapter 6 of the Users Guide - Chapter 6 section 4: labels. There are sections on showing the labels that are automatically placed on the plot, removing an individual label, and placing your own label anywhere you wish.
http://www.ferret.noaa.gov/Ferret/Documentation/Users_Guide/current/fer_html.htm

Look up "labels, adding" , "labels, removing", and so forth in the index to the manual.

Sometimes it is more convenient to use the /NOLABELS qualifier on your plot command, and add back just the labels you want to appear on the plot.
