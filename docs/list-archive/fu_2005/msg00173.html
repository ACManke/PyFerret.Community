Hi ,
The default regridding that is used in

   let sst10=sst[G=xy10grid]

is a linear interpolation. @AVE regridding weights the
computation according to the portion of the grid cells lying in the
requested interval. Perhaps this FAQ will help to fill in the details.

http://www.ferret.noaa.gov/Ferret/FAQ/analysis/regridding_with_ave.html
