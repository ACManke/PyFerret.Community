Hi,

I have some tips for those who use "centered_vectors.jnl". One
problem that I have with "centered_vectors.jnl" is that I get huge
dots at all grid points overlapped with vectors. A quick way
to get rid of them is to commenting out this line in
centered_vectors.jnl:

!set up the PLOT+ environment so that the scalings, etc. are known
!$1/ASPECT/LENGTH=$4/XSKIP=1/YSKIP=1 $2-$2, $3-$3

Plus, in case you want to make your arrow thicker, you can add
/line=13 option in the last line of the centered_vectors.jnl:

PLOT/VS/LINE/D=custom_vectors.dat/@aros/nolab/overlay arrow_x,arrow_y

Good day.
