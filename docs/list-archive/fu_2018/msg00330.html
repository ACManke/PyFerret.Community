On Thu, Sep 6, 2018 at 9:15 AM, Ansley C. Manke <ansley.b.manke@xxxxxxxx> wrote:

Hi,

I'm not completely sure I know what your data is like and what you are doing, but it seems to me that the @FLN:5 filler is finding enough data to fill some gaps and not others. At some timesteps, there are gaps larger than 5 grid cells wide (in both directions), and at other timesteps the gaps are small enough that they are filled.

Try looking at SHADE plots at some of the timesteps where you see incomplete filling to see what they look like.

Ansley
