Hi,

You'll need to subscribe to the Ferret Users List before you can send questions to the list. I have gone ahead and added your email to the list, but you will need to write a new message to ferret_users@xxxxxxxx .  You should have already got one or two email messages saying you have subscribed.

I have not studied your question in detail but I see one or two things for you to try.

If you want your xax and yax to represent longitude and latitude, use the /units qualifier on the DEFINE AXIS command, /units=degrees_east and /units=degrees_north.

Other than that I don't see a problem with what you have done, but instead of RESHAPE, you might instead do a regridding in just the X and Y directions, using the @ASN regridding transformation.
