Thanks, Mick.

> 	Your use of the "ppl tics" command is not in accordance with
> the Plotplus manual.  There are additional arguments for positioning
> the x and y tics 
> 	ppl tics,smx,lgx,smy,lgy,IX,IY
> IX and IY are 1,0,-1 for tics inside, straddling, and outside the axis
> with -1 as default.
> So try
> 	ppl tics .0,.35,.0,.35,1,1
> and see if some of your problems are solved.
> The problem in the lower left of the x and y labels may persist. One
> way out may be to eliminate one or other of them with the "ppl xaxis"
> or "ppl yaxis" command
> e.g.      ppl xaxis,0.01,100,10   should get rid of the 0deg label.
> 

It works perfectly, even the labels at the lower left corner are fine.

Any suggestions as to how to keep these inward tics from being covered
by "fill"?  The resolution with "shade" just isn't as good as "fill".
