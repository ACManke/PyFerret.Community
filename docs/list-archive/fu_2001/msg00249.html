>Hello,
>You can draw the coastline from one of the etopo data sets by contouring
>at zero.   Is this what you want to do?
>
>use etopo20
>set region/x=90w:20e/y=15:55
>shade rose
>contour/over/levels="(0., 0., 0.1, -3)" rose
