Hi Brent,
	There is a plotplus command that controls the labelling interval
of the two parts of the time axis labels

	ppl txlint low_int,hi_int

that will I think help you out.  The "low" labels would be months in
a plot where months and years were the default, and "high" would be
the year label(s).  So to supress the year label you might do
something like
	plot/set v
	ppl axlint,1,0
	ppl plot
which should result in every month label showing but NO YEAR LABELS.
	In a multiyear plot one might choose ppl txlint,3,2 to mark
every third month and every second year.
