Hi Linda,
If you are source'ing the paths from your .bashrc, you need
export FER_DIR="/usr/local/ferret", not just
FER_DIR=/usr/local/ferret.  Ditto for the other FERRET env variables.
Multiple paths require (for example --- one that works)
export FER_DATA=". $HOME/ferret/data $FER_DSETS/data $FER_DIR/go $FER_DIR/examples $FER_DIR/contrib /kailas/user1/datasets/data /kailas/user2/datasets/data" 
Regards, Shankar.
