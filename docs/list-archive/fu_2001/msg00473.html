Hi Andrew,

The arguments /HLIMITS (horizontal limits) and /VLIMITS as in

    yes? SHADE/HLIMITS=lo:hi/VLIMITS=lo:hi var,hpoints,vpoints

allow you to clip to the desired rectangle regardless of the curvilinear
coordinates.
