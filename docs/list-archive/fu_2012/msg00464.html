Hi there,

If  PR[L=1560] is  the start June in your data set, then PR[L=1560:1824:12] is a time series of all June months. Similarly, PR[L=1561:1824:12] and PR[L=1562:1824:12] are July and August, respectively.

Hence the sum of JJA of every year:
let monsoon = PR[L=1560:1824:12]+ PR[L=1561:1824:12] +PR[L=1562:1824:12]

then monsoon[L=@ave] gives the average of all summers
