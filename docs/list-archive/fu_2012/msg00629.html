Hi Verena,
Another solution is to give the axes the units when you define them:

   DEFINE AXIS/Y=0:30:1/UNITS="U (m/s)" yax
   DEFINE AXIS/X=0:15:.5/UNITS= "Distance (m)" xax

And then you wouldn't need the PPL commands at all.

Ansley
