When you have an x-window on the screen and ask to save the command as a gif, the image is saved from your screen. See http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation/users-guide/animations-gif-images/CREATING-GIF-IMAGES for more on how this works.

When you save the image, is there another window blocking part of the plot window on your screen?  What is test.gif? Is that what you got when you ran Ferret without the x-server software?
