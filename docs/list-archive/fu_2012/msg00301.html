sreejith k.s wrote:

> I have 10 SST Netcdf data files and each file contains 12 monthly data
> (from January to December). I would like to prepare a monthly
> climatological SST file. Can somebody help me how to proceed with this?

Use the netcdf operators (http://nco.sourceforge.net). The tool
"ncrcat" can catenate your NC files into one big file:

ncrcat *.nc one_big_file.nc

After that, follow the ferret manual to compute the monthly
climatology: go to 

http://ferret.pmel.noaa.gov/Ferret/documentation

and search for "climatology" in the search box, right top of the
page.  First link is what you need.
